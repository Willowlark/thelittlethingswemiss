    Seamonkeys By Emerlingcosplay & Dudedoescos at Otakon

    I’ve taken photos of Emerling cosplay quite a few times now. Just recently as Jaune in our Team JNPR photoshoot. During said shoot, I had told him multiple times to be “less Neptune”. This photo shoot is exactly why. He’s got Neptune’s cool dialed in so well I really can’t picture him as anything else. (No pun intended.)

    For a completely unplanned shoot these photos came out really well. I had just met dudedoescos through our mutual friends we just started shooting while chatting. Little did we know it would turn out as great as it did!
